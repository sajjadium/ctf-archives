Our favorite adventurers, Hedgehog and Krosh, discovered the internet. They were eager to learn different ways to grow carrots and care for cacti. But there's a problem! Only Losyash has access to the internet.

However, the computer is password-protected. Sometimes Losyash forgets it himself, so he's come up with a clever program that helps him remember it. Our heroes have obtained this program, but they're not very good at reverse engineering. Help them find Losyash's password, young reverser!
